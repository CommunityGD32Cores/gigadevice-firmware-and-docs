  This example is based on the GD32350R-EVAL board, it shows how to use the TSI to 
perform continuous acquisitions of three channels in group 5. In this demo, touch
the TouchKey(TK1 TK2 or TK3) on the GD32F3X0-EVAL board, meanwhile, the associated 
LED(LED1 LED2 or LED3) is on.
